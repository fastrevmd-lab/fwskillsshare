## Design takeaways

- Threat-intelligence feeds convert changing reputation data into matchable security
  context. Freshness and service health are part of the control.
- Built-in and custom feeds differ in provenance, update workflow, confidence, and
  operational ownership.
- Enforcement must define action, logging, exception precedence, and behavior during
  stale or unavailable data.

Verify subscription, connectivity, last successful update, object population, a safe
test match, exception handling, counter/log correlation, and recovery from feed loss.
